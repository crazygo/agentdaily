Andrej Karpathy: 600X cost reduction in training GPT-2 grade models over 7 years

Andrej Karpathy reported that nanochat can now reach a higher CORE score than GPT-2 in 3.04 hours (~$73) on a single 8XH100 node. Compared to GPT-2's original training cost of ~$43K in 2019 (32 TPU v3 chips for 168 hours at $8/hour), this represents a 600X cost reduction over 7 years, meaning the cost to train GPT-2 is falling approximately 2.5X every year.
