AI Programming Reached Critical Threshold in December 2025 - Andrej Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy declared that AI programming underwent a fundamental qualitative change in December 2025. Before December, he considered coding agents 'basically useless,' but after December 2025, everything changed with significantly improved model quality, stronger long-term consistency, and ability to complete large, time-consuming tasks. He introduced the concept of 'Vibe Coding' - a paradigm where AI has crossed a threshold enabling people to build programs using natural language alone. His own work pattern shifted from '80% hand-coded + 20% Agent-assisted' to '80% Agent-coded + 20% human refinement' by December 2025. In one example, an AI agent completed a complex multi-step task in just 30 minutes that previously took him an entire weekend.
